Alright, let's get started with this exercise:

1. Lie face down on the floor.
2. Extend your arms straight forward in front of you.
3. Simultaneously lift your chest and legs off the ground. Hold this position briefly.
4. Lower back down to the starting position.
5. Repeat the movement, focusing on lifting your chest and legs together.
6. For one repetition, lift your opposite arm and leg simultaneously.
7. Continue this pattern throughout the exercise duration.
