1. Lie face down on a hyperextension bench with your hips on the pad.
2. Secure your feet under the footpads.
3. Cross your arms over your chest.
4. Lower your upper body by bending at the waist until it's parallel to the floor.
5. Engage your core and lift your torso back up until your body forms a straight line with your legs.
6. Keep your back straight throughout the movement.
